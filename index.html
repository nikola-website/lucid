About Us


Lucid was founded in mid-2024 by Bapo, who entered the AR2 community from AR1 and connected with key players including Beast, M107onetap, and Malt.


With a shared vision, Bapo proposed the creation of Lucid an idea that quickly gained support.


Soon after, M107onetap introduced Bartosz230, who stepped up as the Team Leader, while Bapo took on the role of Team Director.


From those beginnings, Lucid has grown into a competitive and recognized team.
